Privacy
Your browsing history is uploaded on decentralised storage with AES encryption of your private key. This is accessible only with access token generated from your signature. The access token is valid for 45 minutes. For indexing and search results, we use brokers to generate access tokens using zero knowledge proofs to protect your privacy.
